Welcome to DeesWig

what we do

We make neat and easy wear wigs by Laying out,
sewing, and fastening together materials and hair strands.
We Draw and pin wig pattern on model of customer's head.
We place sections of gauze, lace, net, ribbon, and springs
over pattern and sews sections together to form wig foundation.
We are also known for our effective styling and treatment of wigs,
We tranform your old wigs and give them long lasting treaments.
We give our customer exactly what they want.
